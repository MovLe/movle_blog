### 一.windows中设置共享文件夹
#### 1.选择想要共享的文件夹

![image.png](https://imgconvert.csdnimg.cn/aHR0cHM6Ly91cGxvYWQtaW1hZ2VzLmppYW5zaHUuaW8vdXBsb2FkX2ltYWdlcy80MzkxNDA3LWE4NTlkNTU5YzllODZjYWEucG5n?x-oss-process=image/format,png)

#### 2.右键文件夹，点击属性，选择共享,再选择共享

![image.png](https://imgconvert.csdnimg.cn/aHR0cHM6Ly91cGxvYWQtaW1hZ2VzLmppYW5zaHUuaW8vdXBsb2FkX2ltYWdlcy80MzkxNDA3LWU0NWIyOGZhNDcyMjllODIucG5n?x-oss-process=image/format,png)
#### 3.选择用户，点击共享即可，

![image.png](https://imgconvert.csdnimg.cn/aHR0cHM6Ly91cGxvYWQtaW1hZ2VzLmppYW5zaHUuaW8vdXBsb2FkX2ltYWdlcy80MzkxNDA3LTA3ZGVlYjc4MWZlNzg3YjIucG5n?x-oss-process=image/format,png)



### 二.macbook访问共享文件夹：
#### 1.点击桌面，点击前往，点击连接服务器
![image.png](https://imgconvert.csdnimg.cn/aHR0cHM6Ly91cGxvYWQtaW1hZ2VzLmppYW5zaHUuaW8vdXBsb2FkX2ltYWdlcy80MzkxNDA3LWRlMDE4MzIzM2MzZTAwY2MucG5n?x-oss-process=image/format,png)

#### 2.输入smb://+IP地址，点击连接，注意此IP地址是windows本机的IP地址
![image.png](https://imgconvert.csdnimg.cn/aHR0cHM6Ly91cGxvYWQtaW1hZ2VzLmppYW5zaHUuaW8vdXBsb2FkX2ltYWdlcy80MzkxNDA3LTY0YTVkZjIwMzRiN2VlMDcucG5n?x-oss-process=image/format,png)

#### 3.输入在windows中设置的名称和密码，然后选择共享文件夹，即可连接到windows
![image.png](https://imgconvert.csdnimg.cn/aHR0cHM6Ly91cGxvYWQtaW1hZ2VzLmppYW5zaHUuaW8vdXBsb2FkX2ltYWdlcy80MzkxNDA3LTAwYmM4MDJkMGVhMzRkYjYucG5n?x-oss-process=image/format,png)

![image.png](https://imgconvert.csdnimg.cn/aHR0cHM6Ly91cGxvYWQtaW1hZ2VzLmppYW5zaHUuaW8vdXBsb2FkX2ltYWdlcy80MzkxNDA3LTFkZWNiYzE3ZGJhZTUxN2MucG5n?x-oss-process=image/format,png)


#### 4.访问成功

![image.png](https://imgconvert.csdnimg.cn/aHR0cHM6Ly91cGxvYWQtaW1hZ2VzLmppYW5zaHUuaW8vdXBsb2FkX2ltYWdlcy80MzkxNDA3LTE4ODkzMTIyY2ViYTA2ZjcucG5n?x-oss-process=image/format,png)
